# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues or pull requests

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **sadra.bigdeli1350@gmail.com**.

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers have the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.
